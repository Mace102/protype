How Archer's Home Security Systems Work.

Every complete home security system package typically includes sensors, control panels,door or window sensors,floodlights,doorbells and cameras. Together, these elements will keep a home as safe as possible. Archer's systems and gadgets are 100% reliable and extremely secure.

Sensors.

Sensors, placed both inside and outside the home, will alert a security system's central control panel when breached. That control panel will then sound an alarm and alert either the homeowner or the authorities, depending on the type of security system installed.

Archer's offers all types of sensors to our clients including thermostatic , night, tablet, window and door sensors.
Two pieces make up door or window sensors- one that is installed on the actual door or window and another installed on the door frame or window sill. When the door or window is closed, these pieces should touch and create an electrical circuit.

Floodlights

Floodlights act like motion sensors for a home's exterior,except they don't cause an alarm to sound when they are tripped.Instead,they turn on and flood the area with light. If there is an intuder, the lights may scare them off. If not, the lights will alert the homeowner and give them a good view of what's going on.

Profesionally- Monitored Security Systems


Archer's Home Security offers security that monitors themselves. If the system is breached, it notifies our company to respond. A security expert may then communicate with you to determine the next steps.

When warranted,the company will contact the appropriate authorities in your area as well as your emergency contact on file.
